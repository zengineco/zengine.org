A Grammar
for Reality. modulign.org

The Modulign Standard is a universal address grammar for observable reality — a structured, hierarchical notation system capable of uniquely addressing any node in any domain, physical or conceptual. DAG-OR v3 is its current working form, with an accompanying philosophical paper applying the standard to the Gettier problem in epistemology. The most intellectually ambitious project in the Zengine catalog.
